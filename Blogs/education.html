Innovative Learning Initiatives

Updated June 2023 By Sarvam Education Team

At Sarvam Trust, we're revolutionizing education in rural Tamil Nadu through cutting-edge learning programs that combine technology, creativity, and practical skills. Our initiatives have reached over 1000 students across 15 schools, bridging the digital divide and preparing youth for 21st century opportunities.

Digital Learning Labs

We've established 8 fully-equipped digital learning centers that provide:

Interactive digital classroom
Personalized learning with tablets
Students learning to code
  • Smart Classrooms: Interactive digital boards and projectors
  • Tablet-Based Learning: 1:1 devices with curated educational content
  • Coding Curriculum: Introduction to programming from grade 5
  • Digital Literacy: Training in essential computer skills
"Education is the most powerful weapon which you can use to change the world." - Nelson Mandela. We believe every child deserves access to quality education regardless of their background.
