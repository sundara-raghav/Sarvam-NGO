Our Sustainability Initiatives

Updated June 2023 By Sarvam Team

At Sarvam Trust, we believe that environmental sustainability is not just an option but a necessity for rural development. Our comprehensive sustainability program addresses multiple aspects of ecological conservation while improving livelihoods in Tamil Nadu's rural communities.

Tree Plantation Drives

Since 2018, we've planted over 5,000 native saplings across Namakkal and neighboring districts. Our approach goes beyond just planting trees:

Community plantation drive
Native species conservation
Students participating in green initiative
  • Native Species Selection: We prioritize trees that are drought-resistant and beneficial to local ecosystems
  • Community Ownership: Each planted tree is adopted and cared for by local families
  • Educational Component: School children participate and learn about environmental stewardship
  • Monitoring System: Regular follow-ups ensure 85% survival rate of planted saplings
"The environment is where we all meet; where we all have a mutual interest; it is the one thing all of us share." This quote by Lady Bird Johnson reflects our belief that environmental conservation is a collective responsibility.

Sustainable Agriculture

We train farmers in organic farming techniques that reduce water usage by 40% and eliminate chemical inputs:

  • Vermicomposting units established in 10 villages
  • Drip irrigation systems for water conservation
  • Seed banks for native crop varieties
  • Training in natural pest management

Future Goals

Our vision for the next five years includes:

  • Establishing a green belt around Namakkal district
  • Creating a zero-waste model village
  • Developing eco-tourism opportunities
  • Implementing rainwater harvesting in all project villages
  • Training 100 women as green entrepreneurs
