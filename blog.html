Education

Innovative Learning Programs Reach 1000+ Students

The Trust revolutionizes learning by making it interactive, focusing on literacy, numeracy, and life skills. With volunteers and mobile units, it brings quality education to remote villages, bridging the urban-rural gap with innovation and dedication.

Empowerment

Vocational Training Transforms 120 Women's Lives

Sarvam Educational and Charitable Trust has empowered over 120 women through its impactful vocational training programs aimed at fostering economic independence and self-confidence. These programs provide hands-on training in tailoring, handicrafts, basic computer skills, and entrepreneurship.

Sustainability

1000+ Saplings Planted Across Tamil Nadu

Sarvam Educational and Charitable Trust has taken a significant step towards environmental sustainability by planting over 1000 saplings across various regions of Tamil Nadu. This green initiative aims to combat deforestation, reduce carbon footprints, and restore ecological balance in rural communities.

Sarvam Educational and Charitable Trust has extended its commitment to rural well-being by organizing health camps that have benefitted over 500 individuals across underserved communities.
