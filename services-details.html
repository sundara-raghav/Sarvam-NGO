Education

Education Support Programs

We regularly distribute educational kits containing school supplies, uniforms, and books to underprivileged students across government schools in Namakkal district. Our skill development initiative offers free typing classes and vocational training programs to enhance employability among rural youth.

Special educational support is provided to vulnerable groups including orphaned children and students from single-parent families through study material distribution and equipment provision. Our team conducts village surveys to identify educational needs and provides free tuition classes for students from backward communities.

500+ Students Helped
15 Schools
Environment

Environmental Conservation

Our environmental programs include large-scale tree plantation drives across multiple villages in collaboration with MGNREGA workers and NSS volunteers. We maintain a continuous cycle of sapling collection, plantation, and maintenance activities, focusing on native species that support local ecosystems.

Our team actively participates in government environmental initiatives like the Panavitha Program while conducting school-based plantations to instill environmental awareness in young students. We conduct regular waste management training programs for rural workers and organize community clean-up drives in partnership with educational institutions.

2000+ Trees Planted
50+ Volunteers
Women Empowerment

Women Empowerment

Our women's empowerment programs focus on creating awareness about rights, health, and self-confidence in rural communities. We distribute sanitary kits and conduct menstrual hygiene education sessions in government schools to break taboos and promote health.

Special information sessions help women access government welfare schemes, with particular attention given to widows, single mothers, and low-income women who need support navigating bureaucratic processes. We provide vocational training and financial literacy programs to help women achieve economic independence.

300+ Women Trained
10+ Self-help Groups
Health & Sanitation

Health & Sanitation

We improve rural sanitation through construction of bio-toilets in villages lacking proper facilities. Our medical camps provide free general checkups, maternal care services, and child health screenings to underserved populations.

We distribute basic hygiene kits and conduct education programs on proper sanitation practices in collaboration with local Primary Health Centers that help with vaccination drives and health screenings. Our mobile health units reach remote areas to provide essential healthcare services and medicines.

50+ Toilets Built
1000+ Beneficiaries
No Hunger

No Hunger Initiative

Our food security program provides daily nutritious meals to homeless individuals in Namakkal through a network of local vendors. We maintain a system of regular monitoring to assess food and shelter needs in high-homelessness areas, adjusting our services accordingly.

The initiative focuses on consistent, reliable meal provision while connecting beneficiaries with additional support services when needed. During festivals and extreme weather conditions, we expand our services to provide extra meals and temporary shelter arrangements.

5000+ Meals Served
5 Communities
