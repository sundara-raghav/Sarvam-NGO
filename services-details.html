Our Areas of Impact

Discover how Sarvam Educational Trust is making a difference across multiple sectors in rural communities

Education

Education Support Programs

We regularly distribute educational kits containing school supplies, uniforms, and books to underprivileged students across government schools in Namakkal district.

Our skill development initiative offers free typing classes and vocational training programs to enhance employability among rural youth.

500+ Students
15 Schools
Environment

Environmental Conservation

Our environmental programs include large-scale tree plantation drives across multiple villages in collaboration with MGNREGA workers and NSS volunteers.

We maintain a continuous cycle of sapling collection, plantation, and maintenance activities, focusing on native species that support local ecosystems.

2000+ Trees
50+ Volunteers
Women

Women Empowerment

Our women's empowerment programs focus on creating awareness about rights, health, and self-confidence in rural communities.

We distribute sanitary kits and conduct menstrual hygiene education sessions in government schools to break taboos and promote health.

300+ Women
10+ SHGs
Health

Health & Sanitation

We improve rural sanitation through construction of bio-toilets in villages lacking proper facilities.

Our medical camps provide free general checkups, maternal care services, and child health screenings to underserved populations.

50+ Toilets
1000+ Served
No Hunger

No Hunger Initiative

Our food security program provides daily nutritious meals to homeless individuals in Namakkal through a network of local vendors.

The initiative focuses on consistent, reliable meal provision while connecting beneficiaries with additional support services when needed.

5000+ Meals
5 Communities
Education

Special Education Support

Special educational support is provided to vulnerable groups including orphaned children and students from single-parent families.

Our team conducts village surveys to identify educational needs and provides free tuition classes for students from backward communities.

200+ Children
10 Villages
